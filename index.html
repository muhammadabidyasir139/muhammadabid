My Activity

Melasti Beach


Melasti beach is one of the best beaches in Indonesia, Melasti is located in the Ungasan region on the Bukit Peninsula and is therefore better known as Melasti Beach Ungasan Bali. Not to be confused with the other Melasti which is located on the west side of the island near Tanah Lot Temple Tabanan.

Garuda Wisnu Kencana


I visited GWK last March 28, Garuda Wisnu Kencana statue depicts Wisnu riding Garuda. In Hindu mythology, Lord Wisnu is seen as the protector of the Universe, while his trusted companion, the mighty eagle-like Garuda represents loyalty and selfless devotion. Kencana means gold, and both are adorned in crowns of gold mosaic. The Garuda is also the national emblem of Indonesia and represents freedom.

Sukawati Market

Sukawati market is located in Sukawati sub-district, Giyar regency, this market provides a variety of Balinese arts so that you can get souvenirs, the distance from Ngurah Rai airport is about 45 minutes. Not only domestic tourists, foreigners also make the Sukawati Gianyar market a tour destination when on vacation on the island of Bali

Kecak Dance

Kecak Dance is said to originate from a Balinese ancient ritual called Sanghyang, aimed as a form of exorcism or to repel evil spirits in which dancers fall into a trance. The dance first appeared in 1930, after Balinese Dancer, Wayan Limbak worked together with German painter Walter Spies to create a dramatic performance version of the Sanghyang by incorporating the epic Ramayana saga. They took the innovation on a world tour, thus the dance became popular ever since.

Angkut Museum

Museum Angkut is a transportation museum and modern tourist attraction located in Batu City, East Java, about 20 km from Malang City. The museum is located in an area of ​​3.8 hectares on the slopes of Mount Panderman and has a collection of more than 300 types of traditional to modern transportation. This museum is divided into several zones which are decorated with landscape setting models of buildings from the continents of Asia, Europe to America.
